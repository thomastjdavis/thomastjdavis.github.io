Quiz 1 Solutions

Section 1: Instructions - (algebraically) Compute the following limits:

1. $$\lim_{h\to 3}\frac{2h^2-18}{h-3}=\lim_{h\to3}\frac{2(h^2-9)}{h-3}=\lim_{h\to3}\frac{2(h-3)(h+3)}{h-3}=\lim_{h\to3}2\cdot(h+3)=12$$<\p>

2. $$\lim_{t\to4}\frac{t-\sqrt{3t+4}}{4-t}=\lim_{t\to4}\frac{t-\sqrt{3t+4}}{4-t}\cdot\frac{t+\sqrt{3t+4}}{t+\sqrt{3t+4}}=\lim_{t\to4}\frac{t^2-(3t+4)}{(4-t)\cdot(t+\sqrt{3t+4})} =\lim_{t\to4}\frac{(t-4)\cdot(t+1)}{(4-t)\cdot(t+\sqrt{3t+4})}=-\lim_{t\to4}\frac{t+1}{t+\sqrt{3t+4}}=-\frac{5}{8}$$

3. $$\lim_{\theta\to0}\frac{\sin\theta}{\sqrt{\theta}}=\lim_{\theta\to0}\frac{\sin\theta}{\sqrt{\theta}}\cdot\frac{\sqrt{\theta}}{\sqrt{\theta}}= \big(\lim_{\theta\to0}\sqrt{\theta}\big)\cdot\big(\lim_{\theta\to0}\frac{\sin\theta}{\theta}=\big(\lim_{\theta\to 0}\sqrt{\theta}\big)\cdot 1 = 0$$

4. $$\lim_{\psi\to\pi/2}\frac{\cot\psi}{\cos\psi}=\lim_{\psi\to\pi/2}\frac{\cos\psi}{\sin\psi}\cdot\frac{1}{\cos\psi}=\lim_{\psi\to\pi/2}\frac{1}{\sin\psi}=1$$<\p>

5. $$\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\frac{1}{7+h}-\frac{1}{7}}{h}=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{1}{h}\cdot\big(\frac{1}{7+h}-\frac{1}{7}\big)=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{1}{h}\cdot\big(\frac{7}{(7+h)7}-\frac{(7+h)}{(7+h)7}\big) = \lim_{h\to0}\frac{1}{h}\cdot\frac{-h}{7(7+h)}=-1\cdot\lim_{h\to0}\frac{1}{7(7+h)}=-\frac{1}{49}$$<\p>
