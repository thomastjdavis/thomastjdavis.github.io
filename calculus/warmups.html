9/29 Warmup:

Solve the following limit: $$\lim_{x \rightarrow \infty}\frac{\sqrt {x^2+1}}{3x-2}$$

$$\lim_{x \to \infty}\frac{\sqrt{x^2+1}}{3x-2} = \frac{1}{3}$$
